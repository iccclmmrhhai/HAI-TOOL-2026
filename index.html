App User Manual

1. Data Entry

Enter the Patient ID and select the module. Hospital Day is critical: Day 1 is the day of admission. Day 3 is the first day an HAI can be called.

2. Deciphering Commensals

If a blood culture shows skin flora (e.g., CoNS), the app will only trigger an HAI if you enter 2 matching cultures and confirm the patient had a fever.

3. Exporting for Audit

Use the "Export" button in the Case Log tab to save your work. This creates a file you can open in Excel for monthly reporting.
