CLABSI (LCBI 1, 2, 3)

LCBI 1: Pathogen from 1+ blood cultures.

LCBI 2/3: 2+ matching commensals + clinical signs. Use LCBI 3 for infants ≤1 year (Apnea/Bradycardia).

CAUTI (SUTI 1a, 1b, 2, ABUTI)

SUTI 1a: Catheter in place >2 days.

SUTI 1b: Catheter removed day before/day of event.

SUTI 2: Pediatric (Lethargy, Vomiting, Apnea) + Culture ≥10^5.

ABUTI: No symptoms + positive culture matching blood culture.

SSI Criteria

30-Day: Most general surgeries.

90-Day: Implants, Cardiac, Neurosurgery, Ortho-Joints.

VAE & pedVAE

VAC: Baseline stability followed by rise in O2 support.

pedVAE: Mean Airway Pressure (MAP) rise of ≥4 cmH2O is a valid deterioration marker for pediatrics.

PVAP: Requires IVAC criteria + Positive Culture (ETA ≥10^5, BAL ≥10^4).
