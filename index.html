CLABSI (BSI) Definitions

LCBI 1: Recognized pathogen in 1+ blood culture.

LCBI 2: 2+ commensals + clinical symptoms (Fever/Chills).

LCBI 3: Infant ≤ 1 year + 2 commensals + apnea/bradycardia.

CAUTI (UTI) Definitions

SUTI 1a: Foley in place >2 days on DOE.

SUTI 1b: Foley removed day before or day of DOE.

ABUTI: No symptoms, but urine culture matches blood culture.

SSI Definitions

Superficial: Skin/SubQ only. Must occur within 30 days.

Deep: Fascia/Muscle. 30/90 day window depending on procedure.

Organ/Space: Deeper than muscle. Requires purulent drainage or abscess.

VAE Definitions

VAC: Respiratory deterioration (PEEP/FiO2 rise).

IVAC: VAC + Temp/WBC + New Antimicrobials.

PVAP: IVAC + Positive Respiratory Lab evidence.
